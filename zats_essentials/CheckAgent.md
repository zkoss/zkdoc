



# Check

To check a <b>*checkbox* , *radiogroup*, or *menuitem*</b>.

```java

//check
desktopAgent.query("#chechbox" + i).as(CheckAgent.class).check(true);
//uncheck
desktopAgent.query("#chechbox" + i).as(CheckAgent.class).check(false);
```
